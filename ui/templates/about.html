About

This NFL Elo dashboard was created by me (a student at West Chester University of Pennsylvania) for the purposes of practicing and demonstrating what I've learned about full-stack development. This project loads season data, stores and computes Elo ratings in the backend, and generates a clean website with charts that are easy to browse.

Elo Formula
